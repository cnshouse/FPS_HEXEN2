Thanks for purchase Waiting Room Pro Addon for MFPS 2.0
Version 1.0.8

Require: 

• MFPS 1.8
• Unity 2018.4++

Integration:

• After import the package in your MFPS Unity project, open the 'MainMenu' scene and go to (Unity Toolbar):
  MFPS -> Addons -> Waiting Room -> Integrate
• That's :)
